# Identity
An operations system that combines agent-based automation with mathematical optimization (OR).

**Tagline:** Optimization decides. Agents execute.

# Keywords
operations, machine learning, optimization

## Business Lines (3 independent lines)
Core: optimization and development of small (deep learning) models, plus ML Ops.

- **Research — Mathematical model optimization:** optimizing math-based deep learning models (e.g., diffusion models)
- **Engineering — ML Ops:** spec-driven ML Ops specialized for mass-producing small models
- **Application — Optimization agents:** scheduling-optimization agents that handle complex constraints
